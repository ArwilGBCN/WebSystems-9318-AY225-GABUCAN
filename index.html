About the College

The College of Computer Studies stands at the forefront of technology education, preparing students to become leaders and innovators in the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between academic excellence and industry demands, we offer comprehensive programs that combine theoretical foundations with practical, hands-on experience.

Our faculty comprises distinguished scholars and industry professionals who bring real-world expertise to the classroom, ensuring our graduates are well-equipped to tackle the challenges of tomorrow.

We pride ourselves on fostering an environment of innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. Our state-of-the-art facilities, research centers, and industry partnerships provide students with unparalleled opportunities for growth and development.

From artificial intelligence and machine learning to cybersecurity and software engineering, our programs cover the full spectrum of computer science disciplines, preparing students for diverse career paths in technology.

Academic Programs

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

A comprehensive program covering fundamental and advanced topics in computer science, including algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and artificial intelligence.

Duration: 4 years

Key Courses: Data Structures, Algorithms, Database Systems, Operating Systems, Computer Networks, AI & Machine Learning, Software Engineering

Career Paths: Software Developer, Data Scientist, Systems Analyst, AI Engineer

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Focused on the practical application of technology in business environments, covering system administration, network management, and IT infrastructure.

Duration: 4 years

Key Courses: Network Administration, System Analysis, Web Development, Database Management, IT Project Management, Cybersecurity

Career Paths: IT Manager, Network Administrator, Web Developer, Security Analyst

Master of Science in Computer Science

Advanced study in specialized areas of computer science with emphasis on research and innovation.

Duration: 2 years

Specializations: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Data Science, Software Engineering

Requirements: Thesis or capstone project

PhD in Computer Science

Doctoral program for those pursuing advanced research and academic careers in computer science.

Duration: 3-5 years

Focus: Original research contributing to the advancement of computer science

Requirements: Dissertation defense and published research

Professional Certificate in Web Development

Intensive program covering modern web technologies and frameworks.

Duration: 6 months

Topics: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Database Integration

Format: Evening and weekend classes available

Professional Certificate in Data Analytics

Learn to analyze and visualize data using industry-standard tools and techniques.

Duration: 6 months

Topics: Python, R, SQL, Data Visualization, Statistical Analysis, Machine Learning Basics

Format: Online and on-campus options
